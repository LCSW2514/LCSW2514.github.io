Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of coffee grinders do you offer?

We offer a variety of grinders, including burr grinders, blade grinders, and manual grinders to suit different brewing needs.

2. How do I clean my coffee grinder?

For blade grinders, wipe the inside with a damp cloth. For burr grinders, use a soft brush or a vacuum to remove coffee residue.

3. Which grinder is best for espresso?

Burr grinders are recommended for espresso as they provide a consistent and fine grind size.

4. Do you offer a warranty on your grinders?

Yes, all our grinders come with a manufacturer's warranty. Please check the product page for details.
